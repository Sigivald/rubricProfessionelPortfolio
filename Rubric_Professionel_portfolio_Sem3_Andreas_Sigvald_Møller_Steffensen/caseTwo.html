Portfolio

GetOut

Problem

The Site did not reflect the true theme of the experience that the sites customers would experience. It also didn't communicate the possibility for large groups to take part in the experience. The general design and functionality were also lacking compared to other competitors sites

Solution

Make a full redesign of the costumers website based of user research and new UX/UI design theories and methods.

Roles

UX/UI designer, Frontend developer

Research

The project is started via customer meeting with GetOut, where we discussed the expectations for the redesign, the customers wishes, general information such as their target groups and values for the company.

Quantitative Research

Based of the customer meeting and using collected user data, we used segmentation to obtain a profile for the desired target group.

Qualitative Research

Qualitative interviews are conducted with a total of four people as well as transcription to determine direction of our work with the project.

After the interview and transcription, we used thematization to find multiple themes, which we later compared to find the best methods to convey information on the redesign.

Structure

Based of the customer meeting and the research, we were able to move forward with redesigning the structure of the website.

Affinity diagram

In order to generate new ideas and improve the product, we use an Affinity Diagram to have an overview of changes we need to make.

Content sitemap

After sorting and updating our content, we created a Content Sitemap to provide a visual overview of what content should be placed on each page. The content topics are shown by yellow labels with the topic heading written in the middle and the same with subtopics that are green.

Design

With the research and structure done, we moved on to the design for the redesign.

Design (Website)

For our design process, we made use of prototyping with an iterative process. We made two versions of our prototype, the original one that we make based on our research and a refined version based on user tests. The first version of the prototype, is made in low fidelity with both sketches and wireframes, which then was developed into a Mock-up. The second version will be a more refined mock-up made based on the feedback from a series of Think Aloud tests.

Solution

We managed to redesign the costumers old website with new and improved information architecture, functionality and visual design.

Coding

In terms of code, we made use of the classics tecnologies such html, css and JavaScript, but we also made use of a compenation of CMS and Wordpress to generate some of the site content.

This combination worked by fetching data from Wordpress through API and then being displayed on the site
