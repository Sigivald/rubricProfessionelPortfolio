Portfolio

Taiji og Qigong

Problem

The costumers Taiji and Qigong website did not give the desired result of getting more people to sign up for his classes and courses. Mogen's website was developed very unprofessionally which made it difficult for him to show his knowledge and experience

Solution

Create a new version of the costumers website based of user research and new UX/UI design theories and methods.

Roles

UX/UI designer, Frontend developer

Research

The project is started via a creative brief created by the customer. The creative brief enable us to identify issues and subsequently come up with our different idea's on how to redesigned the costumers website.

Quantitative Research

Based of the creative brief a target group was identified and categorization was done to find objective and subjective criteria

Qualitative Research

Qualitative interviews are conducted with a total of four people as well as transcription to determine direction of our work with the project.

After the interview and transcription, a categorization is made based on the interviewees' answers, which will be used for later analyze

Structure

Based of the creative brief and the research, we were able to move forward with redesigning the structure of the website.

Content Iventory

Based on the content from the customer's original website, a content inventory is structured based on the original content, with suggestions for changes. Based on this, a content sitemap is structured.

Content sitemap

To create a overview of the information information architecture a visual presentation of the navigation on a website was made, as well as a brief overview of the content on the various pages. Based on the sitemap, we structure a new content inventory for our website.

Design

With the research and structure done, we moved on to the design for both the web and mobile versions

Design (Website)

The design process for the website started with low-fidelity sketches and wireframes with small amounts of details and no functionality. It then moved on to high-fidelity mock-ups with high amounts of details and functionality.

Design (Mobile)

After finishing the design for the website, we then moved on to the mobile design, where we added the necessary elements and functionality for a mobile device.

Solution

We managed to redesign the costumers old website with new and improved information architecture, functionality and visual design.

Coding

we also created a prototype website, using html and css with some functionality.

Improvements

There is still a need for improvements through adding JavaScript for more functionality and complexity. there also some improvements need for the css.
